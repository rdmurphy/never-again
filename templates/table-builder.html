HTML Tables, Made To Order!

Never have to rely on another person to add HTML tables to your stories again! Use this tool to prepare a simple tabular data display that is customized to work just like The Texas Tribune web site expects it. It supports copy/pasting of data directly from Microsoft Excel/Google Spreadsheets.

(It can do .csv text too! Just make sure you change to "CSV mode" in the options.)

Give your table a title, fill in the source and the source's URL, select your options, put your data in the left text box and hit the big "process" button. The code for your table into the CMS appears in the box to the right. You can preview the results below.
